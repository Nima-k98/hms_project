    Research Excellence: Shaping the Future of Medicine Through Innovation and Discovery

    Research spans a wide range of topics, each aimed at improving patient care, advancing medical knowledge, and enhancing treatment methods.

    Clinical Trials

    This type of research involves studying new treatments, drugs, and medical devices in a clinical setting, often through clinical trials. It aims to test the safety, efficacy, and effectiveness of new therapies or medical interventions.

    Translational Research

    Translational research bridges the gap between laboratory discoveries and their application in patient care. It takes insights from basic research (e.g., molecular biology or genetics) and applies them to develop new treatments or interventions.

    Genetics and Genomic Research

    Understanding how genes and genetic variations contribute to diseases, leading to personalized medicine and tailored treatments based on a patient's genetic profile.

    Epidemiological Research

    Focuses on understanding patterns, causes, and effects of diseases within populations. Hospitals may conduct studies to track disease outbreaks, identify risk factors, and evaluate prevention strategies.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    What services does the hospital offer?

    Our hospital offers a wide range of services including emergency care, outpatient services, surgical procedures, diagnostic services, and specialized medical treatment in areas such as cardiology, oncology, and pediatrics.

    How can I schedule an appointment?

    You can schedule an appointment by calling our appointment desk at +91 9865476576, or you can book an appointment online through our website by filling out the appointment form.

    What are the hospital's visiting hours?

    Our visiting hours are from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily. However, visiting hours for the ICU may vary, so please check with the hospital's front desk for ICU visiting times.

    What insurance providers does the hospital accept?

    We accept a wide range of insurance providers. Please contact our billing department at +91 9865476576, or check with your insurance company to verify if your plan is accepted.

    How do I obtain medical records from the hospital?

    Medical records can be requested by filling out a release form available at our front desk or on our website. Please allow 7-10 business days for processing your request.

    What are the emergency services available at the hospital?

    We provide 24/7 emergency care, including trauma care, cardiac care, and pediatric emergency services. Our emergency department is fully equipped to handle a wide range of medical emergencies.
